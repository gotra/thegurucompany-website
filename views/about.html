About Me..

Hi, I am Rajeev and I am a java , jee, html5, javascript developer.

In this site I have a collection of technology related blogs which are based on my experiences on various projects I have been working on.

I am currently consulting at OLAF European Anti-Fraud Office as Application Architect, building medium scale applications using Java, Spring, Hibernate, Jquery, AngularJS. Prior to this was employed by Infosys as senior software developer.

Native of Bangalore, India. I was twelve when I first saw a computer, my first program was written in GW-BASIC on a Intel 80286. Later moved to the QuickBasic for a brief period before I taught myself C, which quickly became a language of choice.

I remember being over the moon when I wrote a program to show and capture the mouse pointer using some low level interrupt programming or when I could load and display a bitmap image, after all it was in the time when the best UI around was windows 3.1.

I spent several years trying to write my own OS as it was fashion those days! I finally switched over to JAVA in the turn of the millennium and have remained faithful to it ever since, or at least to the JVM. I graduated from Manipal Institute of Technology in 2003 with a degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering.

I currently reside in Brussels, Belgium with my wife Nele and two kids Maya and Mylo. I work as application architecture consultant for The Guru Company.Always looking forward to embrace new technologies and challenges to build that perfect web application.
